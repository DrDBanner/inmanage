# Licensing & Project Support

INmanage is designed for professional environments where reliability,
repeatability, and operational safety matter.

The licensing model is intentionally simple.

## Fair Commercial Use

INmanage is free to use and built for professional operations where time savings and operational safety matter.

If you use INmanage as part of a **paid or commercial service**, supporting
the project with a Commercial Support License is voluntary, appreciated, and
considered professional best practice.

Commercial use includes internal use within a for-profit organization.

This includes scenarios such as:

- Client maintenance or hosting
- Managed Invoice Ninja installations
- Paid provisioning or operations services
- Consulting or agency work

The model is based on trust and professional fairness.

## Commercial Support License

Supporting the project provides:

- Legal clarity for commercial use
- A proper invoice for accounting and tax purposes
- Sustainable development of INmanage

Support is voluntary and self-selected (one-off or recurring).

There is currently:
- No DRM
- No license key
- No feature gating
- No mandatory telemetry

## Suggested Contribution Levels

These amounts are guidelines, not hard tiers:

| Contribution (EUR) | Typical usage |
|-------------------|---------------|
| 20 – 250          | Small setups, occasional client work, or acknowledgement of the work for private or commercial |

## Time savings and value

We know that price is always a trade-off, especially because Invoice Ninja
itself is free.

However, the real cost is usually not the license fee — it is the ongoing
operational risk and mental load.

If you maintain Invoice Ninja without INmanage, you still need the same outcomes:

- Safe and repeatable updates
- Verified and restorable backups
- Fast recovery when something breaks
- Reliable automation without surprises

Achieving this typically means writing and maintaining your own scripts,
documenting procedures, testing edge cases, and staying on call when things
go wrong.

INmanage is a compact, proven operations toolkit that removes much of that burden.
It makes essential workflows repeatable and safer without requiring a custom stack
or complex infrastructure.

The real value becomes visible on the day something breaks:
fast rollbacks (often seconds when a move is possible), clear checkpoints,
and significantly less stress and uncertainty.

## Future Model Evolution

INmanage may evolve its licensing and distribution model in the future
to ensure long-term sustainability and responsible use.

Possible changes may include:

- Optional license keys for professional or extended use cases
- Optional additional professional or enterprise-oriented features
- Optional, transparent telemetry to better understand overall usage patterns
- Dual licensing models or alternative licensing options
- Commercial editions or add-ons separate from the core tool

Any such changes will:

- Be introduced transparently and documented clearly
- Never be applied retroactively to already released versions
- Never invalidate previously granted usage rights
- Preserve the ability to use existing versions without forced upgrades

The core goal is to keep INmanage practical, predictable, and trustworthy
for both individual users and professional environments.
